These solutions complete your formal proof of the beautiful result: **E[τ] = e ≈ 2.718281828**, where τ is the stopping time for uniform random variables to sum to ≥ 2. The approach uses:

- **Filter convergence**: The `∀ᶠ n in atTop` notation expresses "for all sufficiently large n"[^5][^7]
